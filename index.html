Ethiopia

is one of the richest countries in wildlife diversity and varied landscape verity which ranges from 4543 masl to the low land of Denakil Depression 110 bsl. The total land covers 1.2 million square km. the country is also endowed with more than 80 languages and multi cultural diversity. Ethiopia is situated in the horn of Africa between 3 and 15 degrees north latitude and 33 and 48 degrees east longitude, and has total projected population of the about 100 million.
